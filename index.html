The Herrscher of Thunder - Raiden Mei

Raiden Mei is an S-rank Valkyrja and the Herrscher of Thunder that awakened in Nagazora City, Japan. She's the daughter of Raiden Ryoma, the CEO of Massive Electric Corp, making her an heiress.
