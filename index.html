The Herrscher of Thunder - Raiden Mei

Raiden Mei is an S-rank Valkyrja and the Herrscher of Thunder that awakened in Nagazora City, Japan. She is the daughter of Raiden Ryoma, the CEO of Massive Electric Corp, making her an heiress. Mei excels in hand-to-hand melee combat. With the power of the Herrscher given to her, she creates a formidable obstacles to her opponents. She is able to summon "Kurikara" her reborn dragon from the core of Benares.
